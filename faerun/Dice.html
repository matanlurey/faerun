Dice

Dice enum

Dice add randomness to the game and determine the outcome of many actions.

For display and serialization purposes, each die has a unique name and number of sides. The name is the same as the enum value, and the number of sides is the same as the enum value. Use byName and bySides to look up dice by name or number of sides.

Comparison

Dice are ordered based on their number of sides, with d3 being the lowest and d100 being the highest.
