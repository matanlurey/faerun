ChallengeRating

ChallengeRating class final

A rating of the threat a monster poses to a group of four player characters.

A challenge rating encapsulates the difficulty of a monster and provides a guaranteed valid range of values, as well as a proficiencyBonus that can be used to calculate bonuses when an ability check should include the monster's proficiency bonus.

Equality

Two challenge ratings are considered equal if their values are equal.

Comparison

Challenge ratings are ordered by lowest to highest value.
