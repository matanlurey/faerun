Sense

Sense class final

Special senses that help creatures perceive things in certain situations.

Some built-in senses are:

  • blindsight
  • darkvision
  • tremorsense
  • truesight

Equality

Senses are equal if their names are equal.

Comparison

Senses are compared by their names.
